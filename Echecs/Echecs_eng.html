Chess

Home

Opening

- Develop minor pieces (knights and bishops)

- Control the 4 center squares with pawns

- Secure the king by castling

- Obtain a comfortable position for the middlegame

- Moves played are theoretical

- 5 to 10 moves

Middlegame

- Develop major pieces (rooks and queen)

- Make advantageous material exchanges

- Keep pieces active

- Obtain a comfortable position for the endgame

- Moves are no longer theoretical

- 20 to 50 moves

Endgame

- Activate your king

- Promote your pawns

- Prevent opponent's pawn promotion

- Deliver checkmate

- Avoid getting checkmated

- Maintain good pawn structure

- 10 to 20 moves
